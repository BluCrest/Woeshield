What WoeShield Analyses

Phishing Emails

Detects social engineering, credential harvesting, and business email compromise attempts hidden in seemingly legitimate messages.

Malicious Links

Identifies shortened URLs, typosquatting domains, and redirect chains designed to steal credentials or deliver malware.

Suspicious Messages

Analyses SMS, DMs, and chat messages for scam patterns, fake giveaways, and impersonation attacks across platforms.

Crypto Transactions

Scans wallet addresses and transaction hashes for known scam patterns, mixers, and associations with fraudulent activity.

Token Contracts

Evaluates smart contracts for honeypot mechanisms, hidden mint functions, rug pull indicators, and ownership risks.

Anything Suspicious

Auto-detects the content type and applies the right analysis. If something feels off, paste it and let the engine decide.
